          # Standards of Our Services

          I'm proud to say that I built services which

          • gather metrics for our business to make data driven decisions
          • have sufficient tests to safe guard all major features
          • deploy smoothly
          • withstand necessary throughput
          • alert the right people when unhealthy
          • alert the right people in advance when approaching capacity
          • surface errors, latency, throughput, and other relevant metrics at a glance
          • have sufficient documentation for operation, maintenance, trouble shooting, and future development

          When we finish the MVP phase of the Notifications System, we will all be proud of ourselves for building services with high standards.

          # Smoke Tests

          Smoke tests or sanity tests consist of a non-exhaustive set of tests that aim at ensuring that the most important functions work.  These tests are usually performed on the testing or stage environment.
